FAQ

Questions? Answers.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with startups, scale-ups, and established businesses across a wide range of industries. Whether you're looking to experiment with AI or need a fully engineered solution, we tailor our approach to fit your stage and goals.

How do you identify the right AI solutions for our business?

We start by understanding your processes, data, and objectives. From there, we identify high-impact opportunities where AI can deliver measurable value — focusing on practical use cases rather than unnecessary complexity.

Do you build custom software or use existing tools?

Both. We evaluate whether a custom-built solution or existing platforms will best meet your needs. Our goal is always to deliver the most effective, scalable, and cost-efficient outcome.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary with scope. Smaller workflow improvements can take a few weeks, while larger software or AI implementations may take several months. We provide clear timelines upfront and keep you informed throughout.

Can you improve our existing systems and workflows?

Absolutely. We often work with existing systems — identifying inefficiencies and enhancing them with better design, automation, and integration, without requiring a full rebuild unless necessary.

What makes your approach different?

We focus on real business outcomes, not just technical solutions. That means prioritising simplicity, scalability, and measurable impact — ensuring everything we build actually supports your growth.
