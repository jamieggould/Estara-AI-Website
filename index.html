FAQ

Questions? Answers.

What types of businesses do you work with?

Startups, scale-ups and established businesses across a wide range of industries. Whether you're experimenting with AI or need a fully engineered solution, we tailor our approach to your stage and goals.

How do you identify the right AI solutions for our business?

We start with your processes, data and objectives, then identify the opportunities where AI delivers measurable value — practical use cases ranked by ROI, never complexity for its own sake.

Do you build custom software or use existing tools?

Both. We make an honest build-vs-buy call for every problem. The goal is the most effective, scalable and cost-efficient outcome — not the most billable one.

How long does a typical project take?

Smaller workflow automations ship in a few weeks; larger software or AI builds can take several months. Either way you get clear timelines upfront and weekly demos throughout.

Can you improve our existing systems and workflows?

Absolutely — most of our work enhances what you already have with better design, automation and integration. Full rebuilds only when they're genuinely justified.

What does the free strategy call involve?

Thirty minutes on your operations and goals. You leave with a focused plan and honest recommendations — whether or not you work with us. No pitch deck, no pressure.
