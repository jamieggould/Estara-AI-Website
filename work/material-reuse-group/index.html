Material Reuse Group

Sustainable construction materials — UK

A wide-ranging engagement that has removed roughly 35+ hours of weekly admin through two major automations — alongside a new website, a customer portal for subscribed members and an online marketplace for reclaimed materials.

20–25hours/week saved — admin & document automation
~15hours/week saved — admin input automation
3custom platforms — website, portal & marketplace

The problem

Much of the operations team's week went on manual work: compiling documents, entering the same data into multiple systems, and moving information between tools by hand. The processes worked, but they didn't scale — every new job added more admin. And beyond operations, the business needed a stronger digital presence: a modern website, a way for subscribed members to see and manage what they have access to, and a route to resell reclaimed materials online.

What Estara built

Custom software and automation across the business, delivered in stages. Two major automations now handle the repetitive admin and document work. A new website, a customer portal and an online materials marketplace give the business a complete digital platform — all built around how the team actually works, not a template.

Some implementation details are limited by a non-disclosure agreement.

The outcome

  • Roughly 20–25 hours of weekly admin removed by the admin & document creation automation
  • A further ~15 hours of weekly admin input removed by a second automation
  • A new website giving the business a modern digital presence
  • A customer portal where subscribed members can track everything they have access to
  • An online marketplace where reclaimed materials can be purchased, reserved and enquired about
  • Key documents completely redesigned
“So much of our week used to go on manual work — reports, data entry, moving information between systems. The software Estara built handles most of that now and saves us hours every single week. They took the time to understand how we actually work, which made all the difference.”— Kallie Bell, Operations Manager, Material Reuse Group
