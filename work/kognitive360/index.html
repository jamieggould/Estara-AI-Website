The problem

The business needed a software platform of its own — something no off-the-shelf product covered — and a technical partner who would stay involved after launch rather than hand over a codebase and disappear. The vision: give companies a single place to put their documents to work, instead of knowledge sitting unused in scattered files and folders.

What Estara built

A full custom platform where companies upload their files and then work with them intelligently: asking questions in plain language, comparing documents, calculating percentages, analysing general regulatory compliance and tracking usage. It syncs with the file systems businesses already use — OneDrive, SharePoint and Dropbox — and a full account hierarchy of admins, team leaders, members and viewers keeps everyone's access appropriate to their role.

Delivered with regular demos through development, and extended continuously since — it has been in active development for months and performing strongly, with new features scoped, built and shipped as the business evolves.

The outcome

  • The platform is now a core part of how the business runs
  • Company documents have become a resource teams can query, compare and check — not just store
  • Existing file storage stays exactly where it is, synced rather than migrated
  • Changes and new features delivered quickly, without a new procurement cycle each time
  • Ongoing support that hasn't dropped off since launch

The platform

What it does.

Ask your documents anything

Upload company files and ask questions in plain language — answers come from your own documents, not the open internet.

Comparisons & calculations

Compare documents side by side and calculate figures and percentages directly from their contents.

Compliance analysis

Analyse documents for general compliance against regulations — surfacing issues that would take hours to find manually.

File storage sync

Connects to OneDrive, SharePoint and Dropbox so files stay where teams already keep them — no migration required.

Roles & permissions

A full company hierarchy — admins, team leaders, members and viewers — each with the right level of access.

Usage tracking

Built-in usage tracking so account owners can see how the platform is being used across their organisation.
