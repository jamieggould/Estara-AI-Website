The problem

Like most established businesses, each department had built up its own ways of working — manual processes, spreadsheets and repeated data entry that quietly absorbed hours every week. Tender documentation arrived by email and was handled entirely by hand; driver schedules were planned manually around pickups, drop-offs, swaps and skips; quotes were typed up job by job; and long documents had to be read in full before anyone could act on them.

What Estara is doing

An ongoing engagement working through the business area by area: reviewing how each team operates, then designing and building AI and software where it genuinely earns its keep. Four tools are already live — automated tender processing, an AI route planner for drivers, a voice-to-quote generator and AI-driven document summaries — with a rolling roadmap of further improvements.

Some implementation details are limited by a non-disclosure agreement.

The outcome so far

  • Approximately 30–35 tender packs processed automatically each week from the tenders inbox
  • Driver schedules created automatically — around 10 hours of weekly admin removed
  • Quotes generated by voice under Lawmens' own template and branding — saving roughly 10–15 hours a week
  • AI-driven document summaries saving around a further 10 hours a week
  • Outlook, Monday.com and Dropbox connected — no more manual handoffs between them
  • A rolling roadmap, so improvements compound rather than stop at one project
The work

The tools shipped so far.

Automated tender processing

Tender packs arriving by email are now received, logged, filed and tracked automatically across Outlook, Monday.com and Dropbox — no manual handling.

~30–35 packs processed/week

AI driver route planner

Calculates the most efficient routes for each driver — pickups, drop-offs, swaps and skips — and creates the schedules automatically.

~10 hours/week saved

Voice-to-quote generator

Speak the job details and measurements, and a finished quote is generated under Lawmens' own template and branding — ready to export to the client.

~10–15 hours/week saved

AI document summaries

Document processing made far more efficient through AI-driven summaries — teams act on documents without reading every page. (Details limited by NDA.)

~10 hours/week saved

Connected systems

Outlook, Monday.com and Dropbox now work as one pipeline — information flows between them automatically instead of being moved by hand.

And more in progress
