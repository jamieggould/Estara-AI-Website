Cookie Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

This site is intentionally light on cookies. Here's exactly what's used and why.

What we use

  • Essential cookies only. The public website (estaraai.com) sets no analytics, advertising or tracking cookies.
  • Client portal. Logging in to the client portal stores an authentication session (provided by Supabase) in your browser so you stay signed in. This is strictly necessary to provide the service you've requested.

What we don't use

No advertising pixels, no cross-site tracking, no analytics platforms. Because we only use strictly necessary cookies, no consent banner is required. If that ever changes — for example, if we add analytics — we'll update this page and add a consent mechanism first.

Third-party services

Booking a call takes you to Calendly, and fonts are served by Google Fonts; those services operate under their own privacy and cookie policies once you interact with them.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. Blocking the portal's session storage will sign you out of the client portal.
